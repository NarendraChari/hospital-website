About Us

City General Hospital is dedicated to providing the best healthcare services to our community. Our state-of-the-art facility and compassionate staff ensure every patient receives world-class care.

Our Mission

To deliver high-quality, patient-centered healthcare with integrity, innovation, and compassion.

Our Vision

To be the leading healthcare provider in the region, recognized for excellence in clinical care, patient safety, and community wellness.

Our History

Founded in 1985, City General Hospital has grown from a small community clinic to a full-service hospital serving thousands of patients each year.
