Our Services

Cardiology

Our expert cardiologists provide comprehensive heart care, including diagnostics, treatment, and rehabilitation for heart diseases and conditions.

Neurology

We offer advanced care for brain and nervous system disorders, including stroke, epilepsy, and neurodegenerative diseases.

Pediatrics

Our pediatricians ensure the health and well-being of children from birth through adolescence, offering preventive care and immunizations.

Emergency Care

Our 24/7 emergency department is equipped to handle all types of medical emergencies with speed and expertise.

Radiology

We provide state-of-the-art imaging services, including X-ray, MRI, CT scan, and ultrasound, for accurate diagnosis.

Orthopedics

Our orthopedic specialists treat bone, joint, and muscle conditions, offering both surgical and non-surgical solutions.

General Surgery

Our general surgeons specialize in a wide range of procedures. Key activities include:

  • Appendectomy
  • Hernia Repair
  • Gallbladder Removal
  • Bowel Resection
